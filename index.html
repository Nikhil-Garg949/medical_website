Problems-

The basic problem in the transfusion occurs when the process is overlooked.The reverse flow of IV fluids lead to infections and sometimes deaths. Infections like redness, swelling,chills and pain which can worsen a patients health.

Objective-

Our aim is to create a contact-less intravenous fluid monitoring system without the need for physical contact with the fluid itself.The concept uses a driver circuit that reads the level of fluid inside the bottle and as soon as it reaches to a specific limit, an alert message with a beeping sound occurs.
